About Us

A Web app which is developed for testing the traits of a candidate named as "Personality Traits Assessment", by using Deep Learning Models. Models are trained to predict Big Five traits from a video of a candidate. The Big Five traits include:

  • Openness
  • Conscientiousness
  • Extraversion
  • Agreeableness
  • Neuroticism
